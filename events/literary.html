Literary Events

Debate

  • Individual event.
  • Topics announced a day before the Prelims round.
  • Speaking and Rebuttal Time: 4 mins + 1 min Non-parliamentary style of debate.
  • Usage of notes as per judges' discretion.
  • Participants should be prepared for both, for and against the motion.
  • Rules for the Final round will be given at the judges' discretion.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Extempore

  • Individual Event.
  • Topic given on the spot; 1 minute preparation time.
  • No writing allowed.
  • Points awarded for content, fluency, relevance, style, and humor.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Air Crash/Ship-Wreck

  • Individual Event.
  • Participants assigned a character in a crashing ship/plane situation.
  • 1 minute to think/prepare for the case.
  • 1+1 minute: 1 minute to present and 1 minute to answer the captain’s questions.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Dumb Charades

  • Team event (3 members per team).
  • 1 person guesses, 2 act based on team’s choice.
  • No spelling, rhyming, or codes allowed.
  • Final round at the judges' discretion.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Pot Pourri

  • Team event (3 members per team, on-spot team-making allowed).
  • Multiple rounds focusing on different literary activities (quizzes, spellbee, extempore, etc.).
  • Points based on accuracy, creativity, speed, and task adherence.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Just A Minute (JAM)

  • Individual event.
  • Participants speak on a given topic for one minute without hesitation, repetition, or deviation.
  • Points awarded for seconds spoken, quick thinking, and eloquence.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Creative Writing

  • Individual participation.
  • English as the medium of language.
  • No vulgarity, obscenity, or inappropriate language allowed.
  • Poetry: 4 stanzas with 4 lines each (1 hour).
  • Essay: 1000-1500 words (1 hour).
  • Short Story: 500-750 words with a picture prompt (1 hour).
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).

Literary Events

Quiz

  • Team event (3 members per team).
  • No mobile/electronic instruments allowed.
  • Questions: Multiple choice, true/false, specific answer questions.
  • Replacement of team members not allowed after registration.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals (23-25 January 2025).
