What is Blossoms?

BLOSSOMS is one of the most anticipated events at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), providing students with the platform to showcase their talents across various domains. It is cultural fest where prelims happen at intra-deanery level and the finals at the inter-deanery level with exciting competitions in arts, literature, theatre, dance and music.

Support School of Sciences by participating across various events and giving us a lead in the inter-deanery level Blossoms.
